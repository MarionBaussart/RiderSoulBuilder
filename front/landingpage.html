Rider Soul Builder

Web application, for MarioKart 8 Deluxe players, that helps you to find the configuration that best suits you.


The aim of this project is to spend more time playing and less time sercahing for the best kart configuration.

Features

Sensitive


Get your favorite character or choose a specific configuration.

Adaptable


Choose your kart characteristics between speed, acceleration, weight, handling and grip.

Reponsive


Get the three best possible configurations.


You're now ready to play, knowing that the win is also due to your natural skills.

About me



Marion BAUSSART


I'm 28, student at Holberton School Lille and a future web-developer.


The idea for this project came to me while playing MarioKart 8 at lunchtime at school. Because I was spending too much time choosing for the best kart configuration and my peers were tired of alwayls having to wait for me.


It took a few weeks to set up this project which I chose as my portfolio project for the end of the first year at Holberton.
